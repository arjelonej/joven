My Art Appreciation


Me

TIME PASSES, AS TIME ALWAYS WILL - A SIGN THAT THE
WORLD EVOLVES.KNOWLEDGE IS KEPT THROUGH MINDS,
AND SO IS MUSIC WITHIN OUR SOUL.RAIN BRINGS SOME
TRANQUILIIY AND SIMULTANEOUSLY,HINDRANCE.THE VAST
OCEAN CANNOT BE PREDICTED AS IT'S MOOD CAN SHIFT WITHIN
A BLINK.THE MOON'S PRETTY,BUT COMES IN UNWEPT
PHASES.SUNSETS DOESNT WRETCH DAYS,DESPITE IT
BEING THE ENDING.CATS ARE TO CHERISHED,NOT WITH STANDING
IT'S SHARP CLAWS,JUST AS NATURE NURTURES REGARDLESS
OF HOW MUCH IT HURTS.FOR THE WAG OF A TAIL,
AND A BARK OF HELLO,TO NO OTHER FRIEND
WOULD I EVER GO--THESE THINGS COMPOSES MY
ENTIRETY-REFLECTING THE ABBYS OF MY DISPOSITION,FOR
THESE ARE THE MEMORANDA THAT I,AS WELL,OWN.
THESE ARE THE MEMORANDA THAT I EXISTED,IS EXISTING
AND THAT I,NOT JUST ONCE,FELT.
